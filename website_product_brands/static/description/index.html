Why Branding Is Important For Business?

Adding Brand(s) helps you in many ways than you can comprehend. In short, it can be a vital part of the success of your business. Some points to highlight the importance of brands are :

  • Brands help users to identify and recognize products and the organization.

  • Let you stand out from competitors who are selling local or non-branded products.

  • Also, brands create an emotional connection with their users.

  • Adding brands to the website helps grow your business and increase revenue.

  • Make choices easier for you, as users know what to expect from the brand.

Detailed Features List

Below is the list of feature's website product brand.

Add Brand(S) To Your Odoo Website

  • The module allows you to add brand(s) to your Odoo website.

  • And add new products or existing products in the brands.

  • Enables users to differentiate products based on brand.

Filter Products According To Brands On Your Website

  • Users can filter products by selecting a Brand Form Attribute Filter Bar.

  • Website users can use a brand filter to select products.

  • Brand filtration works well with default attribute filters like Puma (Brand)+ 8 no. shoes.

Create And Manage Brands From The Odoo Backend

  • Create new brands from the Odoo backend and add identifiers such as logo and name.

  • Edit existing brands to update new changes or add more products to the brand.

  • Add a brand to the product from the product page as well.

Backend View Of Product Brands In Odoo

  • In the backend, group products on the basis of brands.

  • Identify non-branded products and assign them brands, if required.

  • Add a brand directly from the product page or add it to a brand directly.
