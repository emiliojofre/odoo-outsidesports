Odoo Website Address City

Allow city selection in Odoo!!

Odoo Website Address City enables city selection in Odoo. The admin can add the city list from the backend accompanying the zip code. On the website, select the state, and the dropdown will show the list of all the cities.

How Does Odoo Website Address City Help You?
Website Address City enables you to add cities in the Odoo backend, so your customers don't have to add the city manually. Further, it eases address adding process for your customers.
When you add cities from the backend, you can only add those cities where you deliver your products. Hence, customers can't add the location where your delivery partner is unavailable.
Adding cities enables you to define your reach and helps you calculate the necessary resources you need within that reach. Hence, you can minimize the chances of loss.
Benefits Of Having A Website Address City
Odoo Website Address City offers the following benefits for your business
  • Allows your customers to add or edit the address making sure that the products reach the correct address.
  • Also, the module allows users to add a city just by selecting the city from the dropdown, making it easier for customers to choose the desired city.
  • This city selection dropdown enables you to add only those cities where you deliver products. Hence, you don't need to take orders from other cities (which are not listed).
  • Saves time and resources in locating and delivering the product to the right place.

Configuring Website Address City In The Odoo Backend

  • The module depends on the core Odoo module 'base_address_city'.
  • Select country and add cities in the Odoo backend together with defining the state.
  • Enable or disable the city selection dropdown with just a click.

Choosing The City On The Website

  • User can add a city or change it while placing the order.
  • Also, add or change the city from your profile page and select the correct city.
  • User can add or edit the city by manually entering it.
